Frequently Asked Questions

How does the WiFi QR Generator work?

Our tool encodes your network SSID, password, and security type into a standard QR code format recognized by iOS and Android devices. When scanned, the device automatically parses the credentials and connects to the network.

Is my WiFi password stored on your servers?

No. Following strict privacy standards, all generation happens client-side. Your password never leaves your browser and is never stored, ensuring maximum security for your home or business network.

Can I use this for my business in Kathmandu?

Yes! This is perfect for restaurants, cafes, and hotels in Nepal. Simply print the QR code and place it on tables so guests can connect to your Worldlink or Vianet WiFi without asking for the password.
